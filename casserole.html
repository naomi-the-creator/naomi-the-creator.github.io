THE CASSEROLE

The Tomato Sauce Casserole is a very delicious meal.

INGRIDIENTS

  • 6 vine ripen tomatoes, sliced.
  • 1 tablespoon mayonaisse.
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and Pepper.

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Place a layer of tomato slices in an 8X8 inch baking pan. Spread thin layer of mayonaisse on the tomatoes and sprinkle with about 1/4 of the cheddar cheese and add salt and pepper. Repeat layers, ending with the rest of the chredded cheese.
  3. Bake until tomatoes are softened and cheese is melted and bubbly, about 25-30mins.
