THE RATATOUILLE

The Ratatouille is a delicious French delicacy.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomatopaste
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup minced garlic
  • 1 table spoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup water
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • small eggplant, trimmed and very thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow squash,trimmed and very thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, cored and very thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper,cored and very thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoonsof oliveoil
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 3 tablespoons mozarella cheese

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Spread tomato pasteinto the bottom of a 10X10 inch baking dish. Sprinkle wirh onion and garlic and stir in 1 table spoon olive oil and water until thourougly combined. Season with salt anf black pepper.
  3. Arrange alternating slices of eggplant, zucchini,yellow squash, red bell pepper and yellow bell pepper, starting at the outer edgeof the dish,and working concentrically towards the center. Overlap the slices a little to display the colurs. Oil and season with salt and black pepper. Sprinkle with thyme leaves. Cover vegetables
  4. Bake in preheated oven untill vegetables are roasted and tender,about 45 minutes. Serve with dollops of mascerpone cheese.
